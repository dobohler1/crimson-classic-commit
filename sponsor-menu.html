About the Tournament

A Tradition of Legacy and Purpose

Originally founded as a Kappa Alpha Psi fraternal golf tradition, the event became the Crimson & Cream Classic in 2022 when Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Inc. — Oakland East Bay Alumnae Chapter joined as co-host. 2026 marks our 5th year as a joint partnership — at our most elevated venue yet.
